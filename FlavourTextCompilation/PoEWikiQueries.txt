UNIQUE ITEMS
https://www.poewiki.net/w/api.php?
    action=cargoquery
        &tables=
            items,
            item_mods,
            mods
        &join_on=
            items._pageID=item_mods._pageID,
            item_mods.id=mods.id
        &fields=
            items.name,
            items.release_version,
            items.flavour_text,
            items.drop_enabled,
            items.drop_areas,
            GROUP_CONCAT(mods.stat_text_raw)=Mods
        &where=
            rarity=%22Unique%22
        &group_by=
            items.name
        &limit=
            500
        &format=
            json
        &offset=
            0

PASSIVE SKILLS
https://www.poewiki.net/w/api.php?
    action=cargoquery
        &tables=
            passive_skills
        &fields=
            name,
            stat_text,
            flavour_text
        &where=
            is_keystone="1"
        &limit=
            500
        &format=
            json
        &offset=
            0
